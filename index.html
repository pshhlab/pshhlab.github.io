INTRODUCTION ☰

Privacy-Sensitive Heterogeneous Hypercomputing (PSHH) lab is led by Dr. Nhu-Ngoc Dao at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Sejong University, Seoul. Our lab investigates technological challenges in the research fields of networks, systems, and communications with the aim of intelligent people networking. Currently, we focus on developing cutting-edge solutions of network softwarization, mobile cloudification, intelligent systems, and the Intelligence of Things toward B5G/6G. Directly contact Nhu-Ngoc Dao if you are willing to be a member of our family. Korean and international citizens are all welcome!

Feature article: Dao NN et al., "Toward privacy-sensitive heterogeneous hypercomputing", IEEE Communications Magazine, vol. 62, no. 4, pp. 24-30, 2024. [IEEE]|[PDF]
